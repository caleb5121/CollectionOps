## Register

**Split.** The primary product surface is the authenticated **dashboard and import tool** (data upload, metrics, trends). The secondary **brand** surface is the marketing **landing page** at `/`.

## Users

Individual **TCG sellers** on TCGplayer who export Order List and Sales Summary CSVs and want profit, fees, shipping, and trends without building spreadsheets.

## Product purpose

Make store performance **legible**: honest numbers, clear date ranges, fast imports, and insights that respect the user’s time.

## Brand personality

**Calm, competent, counter-worthy** - built by someone who understands selling cards. Confident without hype. Never “enterprise admin template,” never toy gradients.

## Anti-references

Mesh gradients, animated hero washes, stacked glass cards, cyan/indigo rainbow chrome, all-caps micro-label walls, neon orange primary buttons, generic dashboard skins.

## Accessibility

Readable contrast on body copy; respect **prefers-reduced-motion**; focus rings visible on interactive controls.

## Strategic principles

1. **Numbers first** - hierarchy serves metrics, not decoration.
2. **One accent story** - teal for signal and links; neutrals carry structure.
3. **Marketing vs product** - landing may use editorial serif for display lines; the app stays in clean sans for density and tool clarity.
